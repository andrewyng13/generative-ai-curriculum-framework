# Generative AI Curriculum Framework

### 📌 Overview
This repository showcases a structured **Educational Framework** for mastering the essentials of **Generative AI**, Deep Learning, and Machine Learning. Inspired by the courses at **DeepLearning.AI**, this project provides a roadmap for learners to move from fundamental neural networks to state-of-the-art generative models.

### 🏗 Key Modules
- **Foundations of Deep Learning:** Building and training neural networks from scratch.
- **LLM Architectures:** Understanding the mechanics of Large Language Models and their applications.
- **MLOps for Generative AI:** Deploying and monitoring AI systems in real-world production environments.
- **AI Ethics & Strategy:** Navigating the complex landscape of AI safety and business integration.

### 🧬 Technical Components
- **Standardized Syllabus:** A data-driven approach to technical training.
- **Project Templates:** Hands-on labs designed for rapid skill acquisition.
- **Assessment Frameworks:** Evaluating model performance and learner progress.

### 💡 Impact
This framework is part of a larger effort to ensure that as AI evolves, the global workforce is equipped with the skills necessary to lead and innovate responsibly.
